# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

FretMaster ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

## Information We Collect

### Audio Data
- We collect audio recordings from your device's microphone for the purpose of analyzing guitar playing
- Audio data is processed locally on your device when possible
- When cloud processing is required, audio is encrypted and transmitted securely
- Audio data is not stored permanently unless you explicitly save a recording

### Usage Data
- Practice session statistics
- Lesson completion progress
- Feature usage patterns
- Device type and operating system version

### Personal Information
- Email address (if you create an account)
- Display name (optional)
- Preferences and settings

## How We Use Your Information

### Audio Analysis
- Real-time chord recognition
- Note detection and pitch analysis
- Rhythm and timing feedback
- Playing technique assessment

### Service Improvement
- Improving AI accuracy
- Developing new features
- Personalizing your learning experience

### Communication
- Sending practice reminders
- Providing lesson recommendations
- Notifying about updates

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Practice data is stored locally on your device
- You can export or delete this data at any time

### Cloud Storage
- Account information is encrypted
- Audio data is processed in secure facilities
- We use industry-standard encryption protocols

### Data Retention
- Audio recordings are deleted after processing
- Account data is retained until you delete your account
- Usage statistics are anonymized after 90 days

## Third-Party Services

### Analytics
- We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage
- Analytics data is anonymized

### Cloud Processing
- Audio analysis may use Google Cloud services
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest

## Your Rights

### Access
- You can request a copy of your data

### Correction
- You can update your information anytime

### Deletion
- You can delete your account and all associated data

### Portability
- You can export your practice data

## Children's Privacy

FretMaster is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
