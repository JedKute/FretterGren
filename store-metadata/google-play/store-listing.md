# FretMaster - Interactive Guitar Coach

## Google Play Store

### Store Listing

**Title:** FretMaster - Interactive Guitar Coach

**Short Description:** AI-powered guitar learning app with real-time feedback

**Full Description:**
FretMaster is your personal guitar coach powered by AI technology. Learn guitar with interactive lessons, real-time audio analysis, and personalized feedback.

Features:
- AI-powered chord recognition and feedback
- Interactive lessons for all skill levels
- Real-time audio analysis and tuning
- Practice tracking and progress monitoring
- Custom practice sessions
- Multiple genre support
- Offline practice mode

Whether you're a beginner or advanced player, FretMaster helps you improve your guitar skills with intelligent feedback and structured learning paths.
